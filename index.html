Biography

I'm a Ph.D. student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), under the supervision of Dr. Ying-Cong CHEN and Dr. Hui XIONG. I obtained my M.Eng. and B.A. degrees in Engineering from the University of Cambridge under the supervision of Dr. Samuel Albanie. Previously, I completed my A-Levels at Harrow International School Hong Kong. My current research interest lies in text-to-image generation models. I am also concerned about copyright issues of generation models.
